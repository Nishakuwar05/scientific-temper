Great Indian Scientists

Aryabhata

A pioneer of mathematics and astronomy who calculated the value of π and explained planetary motion.

Charaka

Known as the Father of Ayurveda for his remarkable contributions to ancient Indian medicine.

Sushruta

Often called the Father of Surgery because of his pioneering surgical techniques.

C. V. Raman

Winner of the Nobel Prize in Physics for discovering the Raman Effect.

Vikram Sarabhai

Founder of India's space programme and the visionary behind ISRO.

Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam

The Missile Man of India who inspired millions through science, leadership and education.

Chapter 5

Policies That Built Scientific Temper

The growth of scientific temper in India was not accidental. Several government policies encouraged research, education, innovation and rational thinking.

📜 Article 51A(h)

The Constitution of India makes it the duty of every citizen to develop scientific temper, humanism and the spirit of inquiry.

Impact: Encourages every Indian to think scientifically, question misinformation and rely on evidence.

🔬 Scientific Policy Resolution (1958)

India's first science policy focused on building research institutions, laboratories and scientific education.

Impact: Strengthened India's scientific research ecosystem.

🎓 National Education Policy 2020

The NEP promotes inquiry-based learning, critical thinking, creativity and innovation among students.

Impact: Encourages future generations to become problem-solvers rather than rote learners.

Chapter 6

Scientific Movements That Transformed India

Scientific temper is reflected not only in discoveries but also in nationwide movements that improved agriculture, health, technology, and space research.

🌾 Green Revolution

1960s

The Green Revolution introduced high-yield crop varieties, modern irrigation, fertilizers, and scientific farming techniques, helping India become self-sufficient in food production.

🥛 White Revolution

1970

Operation Flood transformed India into one of the world's largest milk producers by using science, cooperative farming, and modern dairy technology.

🚀 Indian Space Programme

1969 - Present

The establishment of ISRO inspired scientific research and demonstrated India's capability in space technology through satellite launches and lunar missions.

💻 Digital India

2015

Digital India promotes digital literacy, online governance, and technological innovation, encouraging citizens to use technology responsibly.

🤖 IndiaAI Mission

2024

The IndiaAI Mission supports artificial intelligence research, innovation, skill development, and ethical AI for national growth.

Chapter 5

India's Scientific Achievements

🚀 ISRO

The Indian Space Research Organisation has made India a global leader in affordable and innovative space missions.

🌕 Chandrayaan-3

In 2023, India became the first country to land near the Moon's south pole, marking a historic achievement.

☀️ Aditya-L1

India's first solar mission studies the Sun and helps scientists understand space weather.

💻 Digital India

Digital India promotes technology, e-governance, and digital services to improve citizens' lives.

🤖 Artificial Intelligence

AI is transforming healthcare, education, agriculture, and industries, making India a growing AI hub.

💉 Vaccine Development

Indian scientists developed and manufactured vaccines that helped protect millions during the COVID-19 pandemic.

Chapter 6

Challenges to Scientific Temper

📰 Fake News

False information spreads quickly through social media and messaging apps. Always verify information from reliable sources before believing or sharing it.

Solution: Verify facts before sharing.

🔍 Blind Beliefs

Scientific temper encourages questioning beliefs and accepting ideas only when supported by evidence and logical reasoning.

Solution: Ask questions and seek evidence.

🤖 AI Misinformation

Artificial Intelligence can generate realistic but incorrect information. Always cross-check facts with trusted sources.

Solution: Use reliable websites and fact-check information.

Chapter 10

The Journey Continues...

Scientific temper is not just a concept from textbooks—it is a way of thinking that empowers individuals and strengthens society.

🌍 Our Responsibility

From Aryabhata's discoveries to Chandrayaan-3's successful lunar landing, India's scientific journey has been driven by curiosity, research, and innovation. The future depends on young minds who question, explore, and create solutions for real-world challenges.

As responsible citizens, we should embrace critical thinking, verify information before accepting it, respect scientific evidence, and contribute to building an innovative and progressive India.

Takeaway: "Scientific temper begins with curiosity and grows through learning, questioning, and evidence."
